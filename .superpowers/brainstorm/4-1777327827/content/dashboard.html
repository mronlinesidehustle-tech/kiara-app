Progress Tracking

Reading sessions shown in Parent Dashboard
Each completed session saves:
  • lessonType: "reading" — distinguishes from math sessions
  • sightWordsScore — correct out of 5
  • phonicsScore — correct out of 5
  • storyScore — sentences read correctly out of total
  • totalCorrect / totalProblems — for the existing aggregate stats
  • date / studentId — same as math
Dashboard gets a new "📖 Reading" tab alongside the existing math sessions view.
