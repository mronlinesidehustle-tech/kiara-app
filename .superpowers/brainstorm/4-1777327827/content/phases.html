Reading Module — Phases & Content

Section 2 of 4 · How each phase works inside the session

Phase 1 · Sight Words

5 words · Mrs. Love shows one at a time
Default word list (10 words, 5 chosen randomly each session):
the · and · is · are · it · we · go · my · he · she

Flow per word:
1. Mrs. Love says: "Can you read this word for me?"
2. Word displayed large on screen — Kiara reads aloud
3. ✅ Match → random praise line spoken
4. ❌ Mismatch → Mrs. Love sounds out the word: "That word is 'the' — can you say 'the'?" → Kiara tries again (max 2 attempts, then moves on with encouragement)

Phase 2 · Phonics

5 words · Focus on sounding out CVC words
Default word list (10 words, 5 chosen randomly):
cat · dog · sun · hat · big · red · fun · sit · hop · map

Flow per word:
1. Mrs. Love says: "Let's sound this one out together — what does it say?"
2. Word shown with letters spaced slightly (c · a · t) to hint at sounding out
3. Same match/hint/retry logic as Phase 1
4. Phase transition line: "You are such a great reader! Now let's read a story!"

Phase 3 · Short Story

1 story · 2–3 sentences · Kiara reads the whole thing
Default story:
"The cat sat on the mat. It is a big red mat. The cat is happy."

Flow:
1. Mrs. Love says: "Now let's read our story together!"
2. Story shown sentence by sentence — Kiara taps each sentence to read it aloud
3. Speech recognition listens; loose match (≥70% word overlap) counts as correct
4. After all sentences: completion screen with score across all 3 phases

Encouragement Layer Summary

  • Correct read → random praise from shared PRAISE_LINES pool
  • Struggle → Mrs. Love models the word, Kiara retries (max 2x)
  • Phase transition → unique motivational line between each phase
  • Completion → big celebration + score, same pattern as math lessons
