What kind of reading content should the module focus on?

This shapes everything — the lesson structure, the UI, and how Mrs. Love will coach Kiara.

A

Sight Words

Kiara learns to recognize and read high-frequency words (like "the", "and", "is") by sight — no sounding out needed. Mrs. Love shows a word, Kiara reads it aloud.

B

Phonics / Sounding Out

Kiara practices letter sounds and blending them into words (e.g., "c-a-t → cat"). Great for early readers building decoding skills.

C

Short Story Reading

Kiara reads a short sentence or 2–3 sentence story aloud. Mrs. Love listens via voice recognition and gives feedback on accuracy and fluency.

D

Mixed / All of the Above

Start with sight words, build to phonics, then short stories — a progressive reading curriculum inside one module.
